How do I reset my password?

Contact IT Service Desk or use the self-service password reset portal available on the Ericsson intranet.

Where can I submit expense reports?

Use the My Support & Services portal and navigate to "Travel and Expense" section to submit your reports.

Who do I contact for hardware issues?

Contact the appropriate IT Service Desk (IT Service Desk, IPU, EGI, EMB, or ELX) based on your location and device type.

How do I request new software?

Submit a request through IT Service Desk with details about the software and business justification.
