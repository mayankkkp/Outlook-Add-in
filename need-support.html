Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Contact IT Service Desk or use the self-service password reset portal on the Ericsson intranet.

Where can I submit expense reports?

Use My Support & Services portal and navigate to the "Travel and Expense" section.

Who do I contact for hardware issues?

Contact the appropriate IT Service Desk based on your location and device type.

How do I request new software?

Submit a request through IT Service Desk with software details and business justification.
